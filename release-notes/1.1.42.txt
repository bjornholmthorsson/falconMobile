Time tab gets a Day / Week / Month toggle.

- Week view: 7-day strip with per-day totals
- Month view: full Tempo period (23rd–22nd), Monday-aligned
- Tap a day to focus; double-tap to drill into Day view
- Weekends and Icelandic public holidays tinted; today outlined
- Plus button works in every view

Profile: optional worklog reminder. Opt in to get a push on weekday mornings if you've gone past your threshold (default 7 days, configurable).
