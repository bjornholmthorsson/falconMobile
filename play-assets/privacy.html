Akkuro Connect — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-01

This is the privacy policy for Akkuro Connect (Android package com.akkuro.connect), an internal employee app for Akkuro and Five Degrees personnel and authorised contractors. Akkuro Connect is published on Google Play and Apple App Store as a limited-distribution corporate app and is not intended for general public use.

Who we are

The data controller is Akkuro. Questions, deletion requests, and other privacy enquiries can be sent to bjorn.holmthorsson@akkuro.com.

What data we process

To function, Akkuro Connect processes the following data:

  • Sign-in identity — name, work email (UPN) and Azure AD object ID, retrieved via Microsoft 365 / Microsoft Graph when you sign in.
  • Profile information you enter — phone numbers, address, role, start date, Slack and Jira identifiers, worklog rules, and other free-form profile fields. All of these are optional.
  • Time entries and travel requests — the work and travel data you submit, which is forwarded to Atlassian Jira / Tempo.
  • Lunch orders — your daily lunch selections, stored against your account.
  • Calendar events — read on-device from Microsoft Graph to power calendar-aware suggestions when you log time. We do not transmit calendar contents to our backend.
  • Push-notification token — APNS or FCM token used to deliver in-app notifications. Only collected if you allow notifications.
  • Location — only when you explicitly turn on "Check-in to known locations" in your profile. Coordinates are matched server-side against known company / client offices to record check-ins; raw coordinates are not retained long-term. Off by default.
  • Diagnostic logs — basic backend request logs with no personal payloads, used for troubleshooting.

What we do NOT collect

  • No advertising identifiers. No ads. No third-party trackers or analytics SDKs.
  • No SMS, photos, audio, files, contacts, or browsing history.
  • No financial or health data.

How data is shared

  • Microsoft — sign-in and profile information is processed by Microsoft 365 / Microsoft Graph as part of corporate authentication.
  • Atlassian (Jira / Tempo) — time entries and travel requests are submitted on your behalf to your corporate Jira / Tempo instance.
  • Apple / Google push services — APNS and FCM receive opaque push-notification payloads to deliver notifications.

We do not sell personal data and do not share it with third parties for marketing or analytics.

Where data is stored

Application data is stored in our Azure tenant in Europe (Azure PostgreSQL and Azure Cosmos DB). All transport uses TLS 1.2 or higher.

How long we retain data

  • Active account data is retained while your corporate Microsoft account remains active.
  • When your corporate account is deactivated, your Akkuro Connect record is removed within 90 days.
  • You can request earlier deletion by emailing the address above.

Your rights

Within applicable EU/EEA data-protection law (GDPR) you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing of, or port your personal data, and to object to processing. To exercise any of these rights, email bjorn.holmthorsson@akkuro.com.

Children

Akkuro Connect is intended for adults (18+) who are employees or authorised contractors of Akkuro or Five Degrees. It is not directed to children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy materially changes we will update the "last updated" date and, where required, notify users in-app on next sign-in.

This policy is published at https://fd-falcon.azurewebsites.net/api/privacy.
